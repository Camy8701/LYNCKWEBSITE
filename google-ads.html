YouTube Dynamic Ad Insertion (DAI)

Real Ads Inside Real Videos

Dynamic Ad Insertion (DAI) allows ads to appear inside YouTuber or influencer videos, in predefined ad slots created by the content owner. These slots act as flexible sponsorship positions that can change over time, allowing brands to reach audiences directly within the creator's video, not just before or after it.

How it Works:
Creators define specific moments where sponsor messages appear. YouTube's system automatically fills slots with relevant ads. When viewers watch, ads are customized based on location, device, or demographics. Performance-based pricing means you pay for measurable results, not flat sponsorship fees.

The Result:
A smarter partnership between brands and creators, combining YouTube's targeting technology with influencer credibility to deliver measurable, high-impact campaigns.
