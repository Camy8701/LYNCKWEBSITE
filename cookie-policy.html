Cookie Policy

This page explains which cookies are active on lynckstudio.com and how to control them.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to remember information between visits. We also use similar technologies such as localStorage to keep your language preferences.

2. Cookies we use

  • Essential cookies – required for security, load balancing, and remembering language selections. These cannot be disabled because the site would not function correctly.
  • Analytics cookies (optional) – help us understand aggregated traffic patterns through Google Analytics 4. They are only activated after you click “Accept All” or enable analytics in the banner.
  • Marketing cookies (optional) – reserved for ad platforms (e.g., LinkedIn, Meta) and are only loaded when you grant marketing consent. No marketing pixels are injected until consent is recorded.

3. Managing preferences

You can adjust your cookie settings at any time:

  • Use the “Manage Cookies” banner that appears on your first visit or when you reset consent.
  • Clear existing cookies in your browser settings to remove stored preferences.
  • Contact us at info@lynckstudio.pro if you encounter issues updating your choices.

4. Third-party cookies

When analytics or marketing cookies are enabled, the following external services may set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics (USA/EU data centers) – traffic measurement.
  • Google Tag Manager – manages optional tags using the same consent signal.
  • Advertising networks (only if configured in the future) – remarketing or attribution pixels.

Each provider controls how their cookies behave. You can read Google’s policies here.

5. Updates

We will revise this policy whenever we add new tags or change the way cookies are used. The “Last updated” date reflects the latest version.
