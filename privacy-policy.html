Privacy Policy

LYNCK Studio respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Who we are

LYNCK Studio (“we”, “us”, “our”) is a digital marketing agency and academy based in Germany. This policy describes how we handle personal information when you visit our websites, access our services, or contact us through embedded forms and widgets.

2. Data we collect

We only collect the data that helps us provide our services:

  • Contact details: name, email address, company, and message content submitted through our forms.
  • Project context: chosen services, budgets, and goals so we can respond accurately.
  • Usage data: page views, device/browser details, and interactions captured via Google Analytics when you allow analytics cookies.
  • Support metadata: email correspondence and scheduling information when you book a call.

3. How we use your data

  • Reply to enquiries, provide estimates, and deliver contracted work.
  • Operate our learning platform and notify you about new programs you opted into.
  • Improve site performance and content relevance using aggregated analytics.
  • Meet legal obligations, such as accounting and taxation requirements.

4. Legal bases

We rely on one or more of the following legal bases under the GDPR:

  • Contractual necessity – to deliver proposals or services you requested.
  • Legitimate interest – to keep our platform secure and measure performance.
  • Consent – for optional analytics, marketing communications, and cookies.

5. Third-party processors

We share data only with trusted vendors that help us run the business:

  • Web3Forms to securely deliver contact-form submissions to our inbox.
  • Google Analytics 4 for aggregated insights when analytics cookies are accepted.
  • Vercel and GitHub for hosting and maintaining the website infrastructure.

Each provider processes data according to their own privacy terms and is bound by the GDPR or equivalent safeguards.

6. Data retention & security

We retain enquiry data for as long as necessary to handle your request and maintain business records (generally up to 24 months unless a project proceeds). Access is limited to our internal team, and we use TLS encryption alongside secure storage practices.

7. Your rights

If you reside in the EU/EEA or UK you can:

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.
  • Object to processing or ask us to restrict certain uses.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (for analytics/cookies, adjust your preferences in the banner).
  • Lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

8. Contact us

Email info@lynckstudio.pro for any privacy questions or to exercise your rights. We will respond within 30 days.
